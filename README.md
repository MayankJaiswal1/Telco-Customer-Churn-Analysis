## üöÄ Project Overview

This project simulates a corporate-grade Telco Churn Analysis & Retention System, designed for business or data analyst roles.

**Objective:** Identify, quantify, and mitigate churn risk by analyzing subscriber behavior, predicting churn probability, and enabling targeted retention strategies.

---

## üéØ Business Problem

Telecom operators face high churn rates due to:
* Easy mobile number portability
* Aggressive low-cost competitors
* Unsatisfied or disengaged customers

**Key insight:** Retaining existing customers is 5‚Äì10x more cost-effective than acquiring new ones.

**The goal:** not just predict churn, but understand "why" and "what" to act upon.

---

## üîç Churn Categories

1. **Tariff Plan Churn:** Downgrading from high-value plans ‚Üí revenue loss
2. **Service Churn:** Dropping specific services (e.g., mobile data)
3. **Product Churn:** Switching from postpaid ‚Üí prepaid accounts
4. **Usage Churn:** Gradual inactivity

---

## üë• Subscriber Segmentation

* **Loyal:** Inert, Unconditionally Loyal, Locked-in
* **Risky:** Conditionally Loyal, Unsatisfied Churners
* **Non-Loyal:** Lifestyle Migrators, Conditional Churners

---

## üß∞ Data Collection & Preparation

* Collected customer profiles, billing/usage, and service query data
* Transformed unstructured data into structured formats for analysis
* Cleaned and normalized datasets for predictive modeling and dashboarding

## üìä Exploratory Data Analysis & Predictive Modeling

* Integrated ML-based Churn Index Score to calculate individual risk probabilities
* Segmented population into Low, Risky, High Risky cohorts
